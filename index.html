ABOUT ME

Who am I?

Born and brought up in India, I am currently located in Dearborn, MI. I am determined to help design and develop usable software products that delight the user.

I love user-centered design as it puts the user at the center of the process.

I strongly believe in Steve Jobs' design philosophy "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works."I understand the fact that design is something that is felt and not just seen. I believe that process is extremely important in designing and developing a product.

My goal is to help build delightfully simple products that solve complex problems.

PROCESS

Research
The first step of my process is research. I believe it is important to know the target user, the market and the competition before starting a project. I have experience conducting user research, market research and competitor analysis.
Persona Development
After user research, I create user personas to help the design process. These personas give a good representation of the target user. They help understand the users' context, behavior, attitude, needs, challenges and goals.
Scenarios (User Stories)
Once the personas are ready, I create specific scenarios based on user goals. User Stories are brief statements that identify the user, their needs and their motivations. Scenarios expand on the user stories to give a detailed description of the user's interaction.


Wireframes
After the user stories and scenarios, I create basic wireframes describing the layout of the page and the information architecture.
Interaction Design
Based on the user scenarios and wireframes, I create the basic interactions of the user with the product to achieve her/his goals.
Visual Design
Once the basic interactions are done, I create the visual design of the software product.


Prototyping
After the app design, I create a basic prototype with a prototyping software. These prototypes help identify potential issues with the design and user flow.
Microinteractions
"Microinteractions are contained product moments that revolve around a single use case" - Dan Saffer. Carefully designed microinteractions delight the user and help separate great apps from good ones.
Front-End Development
After the prototype, if the project needs a web prototype, I create it with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery using Bootstrap. In the past, as an UX Designer and UI/UX Intern, I have worked on front-end development.
Tools
As a part of the process, I use Bohemian Coding's Sketch app for wireframes and app design. I use Marvel for prototyping. I am also comfortable using Adobe Creative Suite for design. For development, I am familiar with git and I use Sublime Text as my text editor.
