About Me

My name is Monish Kumar Muthuvijayan and I am from Chennai, India. I am currently pursuing my Masters degree in Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas. I reside in Richardson as a full time student. I plan to graduate in the Summer of 2016 and am currently looking for User Experience and Product Design opportunities.

Product Design & UX

My interest in Product Design and User Experience stems from my liking for simplicity. I like to keep thinking of ways to improve the user experience on products I use on a daily basis. I also keep an eye out for new features for a product that I would like. I strongly believe in Steve Jobs' design philosophy "Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." and hence understand the fact that design is something that is felt and not just seen. I think process is more important than the end result. I also believe that when the users are directly involved in the product development, the end result is much better.
